# Jason Wei

## Overview

Jason Wei is a research scientist at Google DeepMind whose work focuses on understanding and improving reasoning capabilities in large language models. He is best known as the first author of the chain-of-thought prompting paper, which demonstrated that intermediate reasoning steps dramatically improve LLM performance on complex tasks. He has also contributed influential work on emergent abilities of large language models and few-shot prompting strategies.

## Key Contributions

- Introduced chain-of-thought prompting, showing that step-by-step reasoning elicited via few-shot exemplars improves accuracy on arithmetic and commonsense benchmarks
- Demonstrated that emergent abilities in LLMs appear at scale thresholds, informing understanding of model scaling
- Advanced few-shot and in-context learning techniques for large language models
